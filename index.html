About 4 BLACK DM

Founded in Sydney, 4 BLACK DM PTY LTD connects Australia's world-class food and wine producers with global markets. We specialise in the export of premium Australian wines and the import of fine food and beverages from around the world.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in international trade, logistics and supply chain management, ensuring every shipment meets the highest standards of quality and compliance.

  • Direct relationships with 80+ Australian winemakers
  • End-to-end logistics: sourcing, shipping, customs clearance
  • Strict quality control and cold chain management
  • Exporting to 15+ countries across Asia, Europe and North America
